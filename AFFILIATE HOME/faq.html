Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make money using affiliate marketing?

By promoting products on your website using unique affiliate links, you can earn commissions for each sale or lead generated through your referrals.

What is ad revenue, and how does it work?

Ad revenue is generated by displaying ads on your site. You get paid based on ad impressions or clicks.

How much can I earn from my website?

Your earnings depend on your traffic, the number of products sold, and the conversion rates. With consistent effort, websites can generate passive income.
